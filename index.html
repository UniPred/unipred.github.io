Overview

UniPred is a framework for learning symbolic predicates from perception and interaction. It aims to bridge high dimensional observations with abstraction, reasoning, and long horizon planning.

Instead of relying on manually defined symbolic abstractions, UniPred learns predicates from data and grounds them in observations.

Grounding Predicates for Planning

UniPred grounds learned predicates back into the world through perception models and state estimation.

Grounded predicates can be consumed by symbolic planners or neural symbolic systems for long horizon action generation.
